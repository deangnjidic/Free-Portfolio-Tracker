Everything you need to know about Free Portfolio Tracker.

General
Free Portfolio Tracker is a free, browser-based investment portfolio tracker. You add your assets (stocks, crypto, metals, savings), enter the quantities you hold, and the app fetches live prices to show you your portfolio's current value ‚Äî all without any account, login, or server.

Privacy & Data
Everything is stored in your browser's localStorage. Your holdings, quantities, snapshots, and settings never leave your device. No data is sent to any server owned by this app.
Your portfolio data will be lost if you clear site data or localStorage for this domain. Use the Export/Import CSV feature regularly to back up your data so you can restore it any time.
The app uses Google Analytics 4 to track page views only ‚Äî this helps understand which features are used most. No portfolio data, asset names, quantities, or values are ever sent to analytics. You can decline analytics tracking via the cookie consent banner.

API Keys
Live price data comes from third-party APIs (Finnhub for stocks & crypto, Metals.dev for precious metals). These services require an API key to identify requests and enforce their free-tier rate limits. The keys are free to obtain and take about 2 minutes to set up.
  • Finnhub (stocks & crypto): Sign up free at finnhub.io/register
  • Metals.dev (gold, silver, etc.): Sign up free at metals.dev

Once you have the keys, paste them in the app's Settings panel (‚öôÔ∏è icon).
